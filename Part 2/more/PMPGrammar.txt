<Program>      --> begin <Code> end
<Code>         --> epsilon
               --> <InstList>
<InstList>     --> <Instruction>
               --> <Instruction> dots <InstList>
<Instruction>  --> <Assign>
               --> <If>
               --> <While>
               --> <For>
               --> <Print>
               --> <Read>
               --> begin <InstList> end
<Assign>       --> varname assign <ExprArith>
<ExprArith>    --> varname
               --> number
               --> lparen <ExprArith> rparen
               --> uminus <ExprArith>
               --> <ExprArith> <Op> <ExprArith>
<Op>           --> plus
               --> bminus
               --> times
               --> divide
<If>           --> if <Cond> then <Instruction>
               --> if <Cond> then <Instruction> else <Instruction>
<Cond>         --> <Cond> and <Cond>
               --> <Cond> or <Cond>
               --> lbrack <Cond> rbrack
               --> <SimpleCond>
<SimpleCond>   --> <ExprArith> <Comp> <ExprArith>
<Comp>         --> equal
               --> smaller
<While>        --> while <Cond> do <Instruction>
<Print>        --> print lparen varname rparen
<Read>         --> read lparen varname rparen
